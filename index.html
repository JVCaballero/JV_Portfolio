Digital Marketing Landing Page

A digital marketing landing page is a standalone web page, created specifically for a marketing or advertising campaign. It’s where a visitor “lands” after they click on a link in an email, or ads from Google, Bing, YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, or similar places on the web.

For example, you might have a Google Ads campaign, a Facebook Ads campaign, and an email marketing campaign all driving traffic to your website. In this case, you’d want to create a unique landing page for each of these campaigns because you want to ensure that the messaging matches the messaging in your ads.
